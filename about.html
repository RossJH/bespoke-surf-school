Lochy's History

I have been in the water since i was 10yrs old. Currently hold 5yrs surf coaching experience and over 16yrs surfing experience.


During that time i was also a professional Lifeguard for over 11yrs. I also currently coach at Bermagui Fitness Factory.

Group Lessons

During my surf career i have been a resident surf instructor in Byron Bay, East Java, Coffs Harbour, Thirroul and Mentaui Islands in Indonesia.


I have taught many groups from beginners learning to stand up to intermediate that want to improve their style. Bespoke is an Australian Accredited Surf School and i have a cert 3 qualification.

Solo Lessons

Surfing can be something you want to get the hang of before you go with friends.


If you want a more personal lesson to build experience faster i am currently taking bookings.
