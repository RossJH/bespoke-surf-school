FAQ

What are the lesson times?
Lesson times are flexible depending on the time of year. Please msg me on Facebook a few days before your chosen day to organise an exact time pending weather conditions.
Do i need any equipment?
No we provide you with your board and wetsuit if needed depending on the time of year. You simply just need to turn up.
Is there a minimum age?
Generally it is best that any children are of the age that they are able to swim without assistance. Usually 8-10yrs and older for them to get the most benefit from the lessons and enjoy themselves.
What beaches are available to do lessons?
Currently we are able to do lessons at the local beaches around Bermagui except Bears Beach.
Are you available for lessons outside of Bermagui?
Yes however this needs to be organised in advance. Please msg me on Facebook for more details.
What is the cost of a lesson?
Currently we are offering 1.5hrs for $100 for a solo lesson. A group lesson of 2ppl is $180 for 1.5hrs. A larger group up to 4ppl is $300 for 1.5hrs.
