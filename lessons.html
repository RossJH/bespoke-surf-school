Some lessons you will learn during the session.

Lesson 1

How to lay on your board correctly.

This sets the foundation to make standing up and paddling easier.

Lesson 2

How to paddle correctly.

As a new surfer if you arent paddling correctly you will get tired very quickly and no longer enjoy yourself.

Lesson 3

How to stand up correctly.

Learning to stand up takes practice and time. If you learn correctly the right habits it will reduce the time it takes to get up every time without falling off.

Lesson 4

How to sit on your board.

Sitting on your board takes a lot of balance at first until you get the hang of it. Once you can sit it allows you to relax before paddling again.

Lesson 5

How to carry your board correctly.

Boards can be heavy sometimes and awkward to carry. Doing it the right way makes it easier and safer.
